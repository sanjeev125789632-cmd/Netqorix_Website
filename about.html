Who We Are

About Netqorix

A two-person, India-based digital marketing startup — built on real business analysis and data-driven marketing experience, united by one goal: measurable growth for our clients.

Our Story

Netqorix was founded by two tech professionals who spent years working inside the industry — in business analysis, data analytics and hands-on marketing execution — before deciding to build something of their own. Rather than start big, we started focused: close the gap between creative marketing and provable business results for a small number of clients we could genuinely commit to.

We're a small, India-based team by design. That means every client works directly with the founders — no layers, no hand-offs, just accountable, data-backed execution across search, content, app and website development.
